Welcome to Hound!

Just like the dog's namesake, this application helps you track down a person you may be searching for.

Perhaps you just have their name, but you know they live in the area. Perhaps you'll start collecting a list of potential addresses where you can find them. This app helps you search those addresses in an efficient, systematic way.
